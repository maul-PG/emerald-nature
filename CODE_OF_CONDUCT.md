# Code of Conduct

This project follows a standard Code of Conduct to provide a welcoming and
respectful community for contributors and users.

## Expected behavior
- Be friendly and respectful in communications (issues, PRs, discussions).
- Use inclusive language and respect different perspectives.
- Provide constructive feedback and be open to suggested improvements.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Threats, stalking, or doxxing.
- Repeated disruptive behavior after warnings.

## Reporting and enforcement
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to the maintainers:

collaborators) will review reports and take appropriate action which may include
warning, rejection of contributions, or removal from the project.
